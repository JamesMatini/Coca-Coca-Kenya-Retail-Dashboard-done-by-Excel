# Coca-Cola Kenya Retail Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project involves the development of an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze Coca-Cola Kenya's retail sales and performance for the years 2022 and 2023. The primary goal is to transform raw sales data into actionable insights to inform strategic planning and drive sales growth for 2024 and beyond.

## Objectives
The dashboard was designed to answer key business questions, including:
* Comparing total sales and operating profit between 2022 and 2023.
* Identifying top-performing quarters, beverage brands, and retailers.
* Analyzing regional sales performance and year-over-year growth.
* Understanding the relationship between sales and profit margins.

## Key Features
* **Dynamic Analysis:** Utilized Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts for interactive data exploration.
* **Advanced Formulas:** Implemented `SUMIFS`, `AVERAGEIFS`, `VLOOKUP`, and `IFERROR` for accurate calculations.
* **Data Visualization:** Incorporated charts and graphs to clearly display trends and comparisons.
* **Interactive Filtering:** Used slicers and timelines to filter data by year, quarter, region, and retailer.
* **Conditional Formatting:** Applied to automatically highlight key metrics and performance variances.

## Results & Insights
The analysis revealed significant growth and key performance drivers:
* **Sales Growth:** Total sales more than doubled, from **$12.0M in 2022 to $24.8M in 2023**.
* **Profitability:** Total Operating Profit reached **$4.72 Million**.
* **Top Brands:** Coca-Cola, Dasani, and Diet Coke were the leading contributors to sales.
* **Top Retailers:** Amazon, BevCo, and FizzyCo showed the strongest growth and positive variance.
* **Seasonal Trends:** Q3 and Q4 of 2023 achieved the highest sales, while Q1 and Q3 showed the strongest profit margins.

## Strategic Recommendations
1.  **Strengthen Retailer Partnerships:** Deepen relationships with high-performing retailers (Amazon, BevCo, FizzyCo).
2.  **Leverage Top Brands:** Focus marketing and promotions on leading brands (Coca-Cola, Dasani, Diet Coke).
3.  **Optimize Quarterly Performance:** Replicate successful Q3/Q4 strategies to boost sales in underperforming quarters.
4.  **Regional Targeting:** Implement tailored marketing campaigns based on regional performance disparities.

## Tools & Technologies
* **Platform:** Microsoft Excel
* **Key Features:** Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Advanced Formulas (SUMIFS, VLOOKUP), Conditional Formatting.

## Conclusion
This dashboard serves as a powerful, data-driven decision-making tool for Coca-Cola Kenya, providing a clear path to sustain revenue growth and optimize market strategy.
